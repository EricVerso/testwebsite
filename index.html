FairStart: Rethinking Youth Sports

We aim to eliminate the outdated Little League chronological age model by promoting evidence-based, equitable systems.

Learn More

Mission

To replace the traditional age-based youth sports model with one rooted in fairness, science, and development — benefiting both children and communities.

Strategic Plan

  1. Co-Author Research: Partner with academics to publish work that establishes credibility.
  2. Pilot Program: Launch a real-world test of the model with local youth sports programs.
  3. Book: Author a compelling narrative that explains the mission and provides solutions.
  4. Social Media: Build awareness and trust with parents and providers through engaging content.

Solutions for Parents

  • Turn-key system for youth organizations to transition away from strict chronological age models.
  • DIY model for parents: schedule physical activities for their children based on ability and interest — not birth date.
  • Educational media to raise awareness and provide practical tips through articles, videos, and downloadable resources.
