It's Time for a Fair Start

Replacing outdated age models in youth sports with evidence-based fairness.

Learn More

Mission

To modernize youth sports through fairness, development, and credibility — by replacing the Little League chronological age model with progressive, ability-based alternatives.

Strategy

📚 Publish Research

Partner with academics to co-author papers backed by original data and findings.

🏟️ Pilot Program

Demonstrate success through real-world implementations in local programs.

📖 Write the Book

Document the theory, journey, and steps to reform in an accessible format.

📱 Social Media

Build awareness through content, testimonials, and clear calls to action.

Solutions for Parents

Turn-Key Systems

Toolkits for leagues to transition to ability-based formats.

DIY Scheduling

Parents can create personalized skill-based activity schedules.

Educational Media

Short videos, articles, and resources explaining why change is needed.
